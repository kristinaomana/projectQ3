Fire Prevention month is all about safety. March is the fire prevention month because that month showed a high record of fire incidents. Also, According to PAGASA, March is considered to be one of the warmest months in the Philippines. There are many things we can do to avoid these incidents from happening.

image How to Avoid a Fire/What to do When There is a Fire
Instead of using a candle, opt to use a flashlight. When you have to use a candle, do not forget to put off the flame.
Create a fire escape plan in your home.
Store flamabble items correctly. Do not place them near heat.
Be cautious when handling fire, whether that be matches, a fireplace, or a stove. Remember to put off the flame/fire when it is no longer needed.
